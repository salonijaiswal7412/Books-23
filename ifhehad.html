If he had been with me

AUTHOR:LAURA NOWLIN

"If He Had Been With Me" is a young adult novel written by Laura Nowlin. The book was published in 2013 and falls within the romance and coming-of-age genres. The story revolves around the lives of two close friends, Autumn and Finny, as they navigate the challenges of high school, relationships, and self-discovery.
In the novel, the main characters, Autumn and Finny, have a deep and unique connection, and the story explores the complexities of their friendship as they grapple with their feelings for each other and the changes that come with adolescence.

RATINGS AND REVIEWS

GOODREADS:3.8/5
GOOGLE:3.8/5

This book was an easy read. It made me feel emotions deeply for Autumn that I did not know I could feel for characters in books I pick up when I want a “leisure” read. At points it felt that everything she was feeling I would feel. I love it when you anticipate the ending to be exactly how it ends up being. But really it turns out to be so unexpected…which creates emotions and connections to characters you didn’t know you had. That’s when you know you’ve read a good book from a writer who really understands human emotion.-Molly
