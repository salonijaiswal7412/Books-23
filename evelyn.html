Seven husbands of Evelyn Hugo

AUTHOR:TAYLOR JENKINS REID

"The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo" is a novel written by Taylor Jenkins Reid. The book, published in 2017, is a work of historical fiction that tells the story of Evelyn Hugo, a fictional Hollywood film icon from the 1950s and 1960s. The narrative is framed as a present-day interview between Evelyn and a young journalist named Monique Grant, who is tasked with writing a magazine article about Evelyn's life. Throughout the interview, Evelyn recounts her tumultuous and glamorous life, including her seven marriages and the challenges she faced as a woman in the entertainment industry. The novel explores themes of love, identity, and the sacrifices individuals make in pursuit of success. It has received positive reviews for its engaging storytelling, complex characters, and its exploration of issues such as sexuality, race, and the nature of fame.
If you enjoy character-driven historical fiction with elements of romance and drama, "The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo" might be a compelling read for you.
