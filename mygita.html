My Gita

AUTHOR:DEVDUTT PATTNAIK

"My Gita" is a book written by Devdutt Pattanaik. Devdutt Pattanaik is an Indian author, mythologist, and speaker known for his works that explore and explain Hindu mythology in a contemporary context. "My Gita" is one of his popular books, and it provides insights into the Bhagavad Gita, which is a 700-verse Hindu scripture that is part of the Indian epic Mahabharata.

In "My Gita," Devdutt Pattanaik offers his interpretations and reflections on the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita. The book delves into the philosophical and spiritual aspects of the Gita, exploring its relevance to modern life. It's worth noting that Pattanaik's approach is often characterized by his ability to make complex concepts accessible to a wide audience.

If you are interested in Hindu philosophy, mythology, or the Bhagavad Gita, "My Gita" may provide a unique perspective and insights into the teachings of this ancient text.
