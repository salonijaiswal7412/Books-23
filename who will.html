Who will cry,when you die

AUTHOR:ROBIN SHARMA

"Who Will Cry When You Die?" is a self-help book by Robin Sharma, a leadership expert and motivational speaker. The book is a part of Sharma's series of inspirational works that aim to guide individuals on the path to a more fulfilling and purposeful life. The central theme of the book revolves around the idea that one should live life with a sense of purpose, compassion, and a commitment to personal growth. It consists of a collection of short chapters, each containing practical and philosophical insights, anecdotes, and life lessons. Sharma covers a wide range of topics, including time management, relationships, personal values, and the pursuit of one's passions. Ultimately, "Who Will Cry When You Die?" serves as a guide for individuals seeking inspiration and practical advice on how to live a life that is both successful and meaningful. The book's approach is motivational and is designed to prompt readers to reflect on their values, priorities, and daily habits in order to make positive changes in their lives.
