Eleven Minutes

AUTHOR:PAULO COELHO

"Eleven Minutes" is a novel written by Brazilian author Paulo Coelho. The book was originally published in 2003 and has been translated into numerous languages. The story revolves around a young Brazilian woman named Maria, who dreams of finding true love and explores the complex themes of love, sexuality, and the pursuit of happiness.
Maria, the protagonist, takes a journey from Brazil to Geneva, Switzerland, in search of a better life. The novel delves into Maria's experiences as she becomes involved in the world of prostitution. Throughout the narrative, Coelho weaves a tale that combines elements of romance, self-discovery, and philosophical reflection.
The title "Eleven Minutes" refers to the duration of physical intimacy, and the novel explores the significance of those moments in the broader context of human relationships and the quest for fulfillment.
